2017 – Foundation and Regulatory Alignment

  • Launch of the irAE Program by CEORT's Project Data Sphere
  • FDA Oncology Center of Excellence issues Letter of Support
  • Initial scoping of irAE phenotypes and infrastructure gaps

2018 – Early Clinical Focus and Field Definition

  • Immune-Related Myocarditis Workshop (Dec 2017)
  • Consensus publication in The Oncologist (2018)
  • Expansion of collaborative engagement with specialists

2019 – Regulatory and Research-Driven Scaling

  • FDA–PDS irAE Symposium
  • Initiation of Delphi-based consensus processes
  • Mapping of knowledge gaps across regulatory reporting

2020-2021 – Definition Development and Real-World Infrastructure

  • Structured workgroups for consensus definitions
  • Emphasis on real-world evidence strategy
  • First JITC publication (2021): Neurologic irAE definitions

2022-2023 – Standardization and Coding Advancement

  • Finalization of ICD-10-CM and MedDRA coding updates
  • Crosswalk development for trial CRFs
  • Engagement with payers, clinicians, and regulators

2024 – Institutionalization and Ecosystem Expansion

  • irAE Consortium Think Tank at Cleveland Clinic
  • Formation of ASPIRE and STORIES entities
  • JCO publication on AI/LLM-based irAE detection
  • Approval of 4 new ICD-10-CM codes for irAEs

2025 – Recognition and Formal Integration

  • ASCO designates ASPIRE as a Community of Practice
  • ASPIRE session at ASCO Annual Meeting (May 30)
  • Ongoing work on CRF harmonization, biomarkers, and AI tools
